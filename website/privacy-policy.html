Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

Effective Date: January 14, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to AI Multi-Window Chat ("the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using AI Multi-Window Chat, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

The Extension is developed and maintained as an open-source project available at GitHub.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Data

The Extension collects the following data that you explicitly provide:

  • API Configuration: API keys, API endpoints (base URLs), and model names for OpenAI-compatible services
  • Chat History: All conversations conducted through the Extension, including user messages and AI responses
  • User Prompts: Custom system prompts created and saved by users
  • Settings and Preferences: Language preferences, default configurations, and UI settings

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

The Extension does not automatically collect any telemetry, analytics, or usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Data

2.1 Core Functionality

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • API Communication: Transmitting your messages to configured OpenAI-compatible API endpoints
  • Conversation Management: Storing and organizing chat history for resuming and viewing conversations
  • Configuration Management: Saving your API settings and preferences for future use
  • Export Functionality: Generating downloadable copies of your conversations (Markdown/Text format)

2.2 No Secondary Use

We do not use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Analytics or tracking
  • Selling or sharing with third parties
  • Training AI models
  • Any purpose other than the core functionality described above

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage Only

All data collected by the Extension is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Specifically:

  • Data is stored in your browser's local storage
  • Data never leaves your device except when:
    • Sending messages to configured API endpoints
    • Exporting conversations to local files
    • Importing configuration files from your device

3.2 No Cloud Servers

The Extension does not use any cloud storage, databases, or external servers owned by the developer. All data remains on your device.

3.3 API Communication

When you send a message:

  1. The Extension forwards your message to the API endpoint you configured (e.g., https://api.openai.com/v1)
  2. The API key you provided is used for authentication
  3. The response is stored locally in your browser

The Extension does not proxy, intercept, or record these communications beyond local storage.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

4.2 Required Third-Party Services

You may configure the Extension to use third-party AI services (e.g., OpenAI, DeepSeek, Qwen, or other OpenAI-compatible APIs). When you use these services:

  • Your messages and API key are sent directly to the API endpoint you configure
  • These services are governed by their own privacy policies
  • The Extension has no control over how these third-party services handle your data
You are responsible for reviewing the privacy policies of any AI services you choose to use.

4.3 Open-Source Code

The Extension's source code is publicly available on GitHub. This does not expose your personal data, as all data is stored locally on your device.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Data Retention

Your data is retained indefinitely in your browser's local storage until you choose to delete it.

5.2 Deleting Your Data

You can delete your data at any time using the following methods:

Individual Conversations:

  • Open the Extension popup → History tab
  • Click "Delete" next to any conversation

All Chat History:

  • Open the Extension popup → History tab
  • Click "Clear History" button

Configuration and Prompts:

  • Open the Extension popup → Config/Prompts tab
  • Delete individual profiles or prompts

Complete Data Removal:

  • Uninstall the Extension from your browser (this removes all stored data)

5.3 Export Before Deletion

Before deleting data, you can export your conversations:

  • Open the Extension popup → History tab
  • Click "View" on a conversation → "Export"
  • Or click "Export All" to export all conversations

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored in your browser's storage, protected by browser security mechanisms
  • No Transmission: API keys and conversations are not transmitted to any servers other than configured API endpoints
  • Open Source: Code is publicly available for security auditing

6.2 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your API keys confidential
  • Not sharing your exported configuration files with unauthorized parties
  • Securing your device and browser with passwords/biometrics
  • Backing up important conversations before deletion

6.3 Security Limitations

Please note that:

  • Anyone with access to your device and browser may view your stored data
  • Browser extensions can be accessed by other extensions in certain circumstances
  • We recommend not using this Extension on shared or public devices

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to us, please contact us, and we will delete such data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: View all data stored by the Extension through the popup interface
  • Deletion: Delete any or all data as described in Section 5
  • Export: Export your conversations to local files
  • Portability: Export configuration files for backup or transfer

10. International Data Transfers

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transfer data across international borders. However, when you use third-party AI services, your data may be transferred to the jurisdictions where those services operate. Please review the privacy policies of any AI services you use.

11. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected
  • The right to delete personal information
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable, as we do not sell data)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, use the deletion functions within the Extension or uninstall it to remove all data.

12. GDPR Compliance (EU Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: You can access all your data through the Extension UI
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit your configurations and prompts
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all data as described in Section 5
  • Right to Portability: You can export your conversations and configurations
  • Right to Object: You can uninstall the Extension at any time
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can stop using the Extension without uninstalling

Data Controller: This Extension is developed by an open-source community. For GDPR inquiries, please open an issue on GitHub.

Legal Basis for Processing: Your data is processed based on your consent when you install and use the Extension.

Data Retention: Data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the Extension.

13. Contact Information

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please:

  1. Open an Issue: Visit our GitHub Issues Page and create a new issue with the "privacy" label
  2. Email: If email contact is provided in the repository, use that address
  3. GitHub Discussions: Start a discussion in the GitHub Discussions section

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

14. Third-Party Links and Services

The Extension may allow you to configure third-party AI services. These services are not owned or controlled by us. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use:

  • OpenAI: Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek: Privacy Policy (if applicable)
  • Qwen/Alibaba: Privacy Policy (if applicable)
  • Other OpenAI-compatible services: Please refer to their respective privacy policies

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

15. Updates and Notifications

The Extension does not include auto-update mechanisms beyond Chrome's standard extension update system. Changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on GitHub. We encourage you to check for updates periodically.
